Delete Your TNPSC Nova AI Account

If you wish to permanently delete your TNPSC Nova AI account and associated personal data, you may request account deletion by contacting our support team.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to tnpscnovaai.review@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject: Delete My TNPSC Nova AI Account.
  3. Include your registered email address or username.

What Happens After Your Request?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your login credentials will be removed.
  • Your learning progress and test history will be deleted.
  • Your personal information stored in our database will be removed where applicable.

Retention Period

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Some information may be retained temporarily if required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.
