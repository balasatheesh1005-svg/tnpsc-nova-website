Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At TNPSC Nova AI, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and safeguard your data.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

TNPSC Nova AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored and protected when you use our website, mobile application and related services.

By using TNPSC Nova AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use TNPSC Nova AI, we may collect the following information.

  • Your name or username.
  • Email address used for account creation.
  • Login and authentication information.
  • Learning progress, test scores, study history and achievements.
  • Device information such as browser type, operating system and application version.
  • Technical logs required for maintaining platform security and improving performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide and improve TNPSC Nova AI services.

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide AI-powered learning features.
  • Save your learning progress.
  • Generate personalized analytics and recommendations.
  • Improve application performance and reliability.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Protect the platform from misuse, fraud or unauthorized access.

4. User Authentication

TNPSC Nova AI uses a secure authentication system to manage user accounts. Authentication information is processed securely, and passwords are never stored in plain text.

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable security practices, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

TNPSC Nova AI may use cookies or local storage technologies to remember user preferences, maintain login sessions, and improve your experience.

These technologies do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for providing our services.

7. Sharing of Information

TNPSC Nova AI does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared when required by law, to protect our legal rights, or when necessary to provide our services through trusted partners.

8. Third-Party Services

TNPSC Nova AI may use trusted third-party services to provide authentication, cloud storage, analytics, and application hosting.

  • Supabase (Authentication & Database)
  • Google Play Services (Android Distribution)
  • Cloudflare (Website Hosting)
  • Streamlit Cloud (Web Application Hosting)

These providers maintain their own privacy policies and security standards.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Update or correct your account details.
  • Request deletion of your account, subject to applicable legal requirements.
  • Contact us regarding privacy-related concerns.

10. Children's Privacy

TNPSC Nova AI is intended primarily for students preparing for competitive examinations.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such information is identified, it will be removed within a reasonable period.

11. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

If you request account deletion, your information will be removed or anonymized within a reasonable period, unless retention is required by law.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements, legal requirements, or new platform features.

The latest version will always be available on this page, along with the updated effective date.

Supabase is used for secure user authentication, profile management and cloud database services. User credentials are processed securely through Supabase Authentication.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us.
